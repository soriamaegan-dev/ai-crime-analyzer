## What Does It Do?

The AI Law Enforcement & Crime Analyzer is a free, open-source AI-powered tool that helps law enforcement agencies, security firms, and crime analysts analyze crime data in minutes. Upload your crime incident data, case files, offender records, or staffing data and the AI instantly generates comprehensive analysis including crime pattern detection, predictive risk scoring, case correlations, recidivism assessment, resource optimization, and professional reports. What used to take hours of manual analysis now takes minutes.

---

## 9 Powerful Tools in One App

### 📊 Data Upload & Overview
Upload CSV, Excel, PDF, or JSON crime data files and get an instant AI-generated overview of your dataset. Identifies data quality issues, missing values, geographic coverage, date ranges, and crime categories — and recommends the best analyses to run next.

### 🔍 Crime Pattern Analysis
Upload crime incident data and the AI identifies crime hotspots by location, analyzes time-based patterns by day, week, month, and season, breaks down crime type frequency and trends, and delivers a professional written summary with key findings and patrol recommendations.

### 🔮 Predictive Analysis
Upload historical crime data and the AI predicts likely future crime hotspots, assigns risk scores by neighborhood or zone, identifies high risk time periods, and delivers specific patrol deployment suggestions and resource allocation recommendations with probability scores.

### 🔗 Case Correlation
Upload multiple incident reports or case files and the AI identifies links between cases, detects similar modus operandi, finds geographic and timeline connections, flags serial incident indicators, and delivers a prioritized investigation recommendation report.

### 📋 Recidivism Analysis
Upload offender data and the AI calculates recidivism risk scores, identifies key risk factors, evaluates rehabilitation program effectiveness, and delivers intervention recommendations based on research-backed criminology standards.

### 🚓 Resource Optimization
Upload staffing and incident data and the AI recommends optimal patrol deployment, analyzes response times, identifies over and under-resourced zones, and delivers a budget optimization and staffing level analysis with a prioritized implementation plan.

### 📈 Visualizations & Maps
Auto-generates interactive charts from your uploaded data including bar charts, line charts, pie charts, scatter plots, heatmaps, box plots, histograms, and density heatmaps. Select any columns to visualize using Plotly interactive charts.

### 📄 Reports & Presentations
Generates a full PowerPoint presentation, PDF report, or executive briefing directly from your crime data analysis. Professional law enforcement formatting with all AI insights included. Download with one click.

### 💬 AI Data Chat
Ask questions about your uploaded crime data in plain English. Get answers, SQL queries, Python code, and deeper insights on demand. Conversation history maintained throughout your session.

## ⏱️ How Long Does It Take?

Please allow 1-2 minutes for results to generate.

The AI is performing multiple complex tasks simultaneously including:

- Cross-referencing criminology standards and benchmarks
